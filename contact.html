Frequently Asked Questions

What are your enrollment requirements?

We accept children from 4 months to Grade 7. For enrollment, we require:

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Immunization records
  • Medical history form
  • Emergency contact information
What are your fees and payment options?

Our fees vary based on the program and age group. We offer:

  • Monthly payment plans
  • Sibling discounts
  • Full-time and part-time options
  • Aftercare-only packages

Please contact us for a detailed fee schedule.

Do you provide meals for children?

Yes, we provide balanced, nutritious meals throughout the day:

  • Breakfast
  • Morning snack
  • Lunch
  • Afternoon snack
  • Late snack for aftercare children

Our menu is available at reception and accommodates dietary restrictions.

What safety measures do you have in place?

Child safety is our top priority. Our facilities include:

  • Secure access control with biometric entry
  • 24-hour CCTV surveillance
  • First-aid certified staff
  • Regular safety drills
  • Childproofed environments
  • Strict pickup authorization procedures
Do you offer holiday programs?

Yes, we offer special holiday programs during school breaks. Our holiday program includes:

  • Themed activities and projects
  • Educational field trips
  • Arts and crafts workshops
  • Sports and outdoor activities
  • Special guests and entertainment

Registration for holiday programs opens 4 weeks before each break.
