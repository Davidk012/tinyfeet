Our Story

Discover the passion and dedication behind Tiny Feet ECD and Aftercare

Welcome to Tiny Feet

Founded in 2010, Tiny Feet ECD and Aftercare has been a cornerstone of early childhood education in Kibler Park for over a decade. What began as a small home-based daycare has grown into a fully accredited educational center serving children from infancy through primary school.

Our founder, Sarah Johnson, started Tiny Feet with a simple vision: to create a nurturing environment where children could learn, grow, and develop in a safe, stimulating setting. With a background in early childhood education and a passion for child development, Sarah built Tiny Feet on the principles of love, respect, and quality education.

Today, our center occupies a purpose-built facility designed specifically for young learners. Our bright, spacious classrooms are equipped with age-appropriate learning materials and outdoor play areas that encourage exploration and physical development.

At Tiny Feet, we believe that every child deserves the best possible start in life. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to create an environment where children feel valued, respected, and excited to learn.

Our Mission & Philosophy

Our mission is to provide exceptional early childhood education and care that nurtures each child's unique potential while supporting families in our community.

We believe that the early years are the most critical for development, and our approach focuses on creating a foundation for lifelong learning through play, exploration, and positive relationships.

Our educational philosophy is inspired by the Reggio Emilia approach, which views children as capable, curious learners with their own unique perspectives. We create environments that encourage discovery, creativity, and collaboration.

At Tiny Feet, we focus on the whole child - supporting their cognitive, social, emotional, and physical development through carefully designed learning experiences tailored to each age group.

Our Journey

2010

Tiny Feet began as a small home daycare with just 5 children in Sarah Johnson's residence.

2012

Moved to our first dedicated facility on Gordon Road, expanding to accommodate 30 children.

2015

Received full accreditation from the Department of Social Development.

2017

Launched our Aftercare Program for primary school children.

2020

Completed major renovations to expand our facilities and create specialized learning zones.

2023

Celebrated 13 years of service to the Kibler Park community with over 200 alumni.
