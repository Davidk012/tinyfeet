About Our Centre

Welcome to Tiny Feet ECD Centre and Aftercare, where we provide exceptional care and early childhood development for children in Kibler Park and surrounding areas.

Our dedicated team of qualified educators creates a nurturing environment that fosters growth, learning, and creativity for children from 4 months to Grade R.

We also offer comprehensive aftercare services for school-going children from Grade 1 to Grade 7, ensuring they have a safe, supportive space to complete homework, engage in activities, and socialize after school hours.

At Tiny Feet, we believe in holistic development and provide nutritious meals throughout the day to support your child's growth and energy needs.

Our Services

Early Childhood Development

We enroll children from 4 months to Grade R, providing age-appropriate learning activities that stimulate cognitive, emotional, and physical development in a safe, nurturing environment.

Aftercare Program

Our aftercare program serves learners from Grade 1 to Grade 7. We provide homework assistance, supervised play, and engaging activities in a structured, supportive setting.

Nutritious Meals

We provide balanced, nutritious meals throughout the day including breakfast, morning snack, lunch, afternoon snack, and late snack. Our menu is available at reception.
